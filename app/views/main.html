Infinity Sort

Welcome to Infinity Sort! This app is a platform to do affinity diagramming. With infinity sort, affinity diagramming is no longer restricted by physical whitebords and you can share and collaborate in a super convenient way. Your diagrams will be stored in a folder names "infinity-sort" in your Dropbox. You can share your diagrams with your colleagues by sharing the folder.

In order for this app to work, Please enable pop-ups on this site. This is just so Dropbox can connect properly.

Press the big green button to get started!
